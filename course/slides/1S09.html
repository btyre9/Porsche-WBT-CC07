Writing an Email a Customer Will Trust

Email is your written record. Five details make the difference between professional and amateur.

  • Write a clear, descriptive subject line — "Annual service findings — your 2022 Taycan" beats "Update"
  • Open with a proper greeting using the customer's name
  • Use short paragraphs or bullets — never a wall of text
  • Proofread before sending — autocorrect mistakes survive forever in email
  • End with clear next steps and your contact info in the signature
Why It WorksRead the email aloud before you send it. Tone you can hear is tone you can fix.
