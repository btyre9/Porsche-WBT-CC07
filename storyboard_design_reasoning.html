Module 3: Behavioral Styles — Reading Your Customer

Why Exactly 11 Content Slides?

Instructional design at Porsche mandates that a standard WBT module target 15–20 minutes of active learning seat time. To cover the DiSC framework thoroughly without causing cognitive overload or unnecessary scrolling, the AI mapped Module 3's information outline across a curated 11 content slides.

  • 4

    Learning Objectives covered

    Each learning objective gets a tailored intro, active conceptual framing, real-world examples, and interactive application slides.

  • 3-4

    Slides per major theme axis

    Ensures that complex details (verbal cues vs. video adaptations) get dedicated layouts instead of forcing the learner to scan massive single-slide texts.

  • 80%

    Interactive core slides

    Uses card-explore and tab-panel formats to force user clicking, triggering focused micro-recordings to ensure high retention scores.

Slide-by-Slide Design Rationale

Slide 01: Opener hero-title

Establishes Porsche brand context, links behavioral alignment to Gold-Certified technician professionalism, and builds learner anticipation.

Slide 02: Objectives learning-objectives

Fades in the 4 learning objectives sequentially. Built-in interactive lock restricts progression until the objectives VO is finished.

Slide 03: Why DiSC? content-split

Conveys Objective 1. Highlights that "different does not mean wrong" and frames profiling as a respect-based relationship accelerator.

Slide 04: The 4 Styles card-explore

Conveys Objective 2. Employs a beautiful card-explore grid for Dominance, Influence, Steadiness, and Conscientiousness to make spotting easy.

Slide 05: Spotting Cues tab-panel

Conveys Objective 3. Segregates verbal, pace, and non-verbal cues into tab selectors so technicians learn drive-path dynamics.

Slide 06: Elevator Test content-split

Conveys Objective 3. Employs a memorable visual elevator lobby scenario to ground style attributes before the mid-course checks.

Slide 07: Adapting Explanations card-explore

Conveys Objective 4. Explains precisely how to talk to each style (Direct D, Warm I, Reassuring S, Analytical C) during recommendations.

Slide 08: Scenarios tab-panel

Conveys Objective 5. Anchors behavioral adaptions to critical daily jobs like Video Updates, Cost structures, and Procedural Timelines.

Slide 09: Priorities content-bullets

Fine-tunes communication preferences by introducing the 8 specialized Priorities within the traditional DiSC circle.

Slide 10: Meeting Customers content-quote

Provides a dramatic, emotionally resonant pause centered around the core philosophy of FLEXING to serve and build lasting partnerships.

Slide 11: Summary closing

Recaps the module competencies and prepares the learner with confidence before unlocking the randomized final assessment.
